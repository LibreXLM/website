What is DAWG?

DAWG is an experimental reserve meme coin and store-of-value (SoV) token being built inside the LIBRE Trading Ecosystem on Stellar. The core idea is simple:

DAWG should be economically backed by LIBRE and a growing on-chain Treasury, with the initial policy target being approximately 1 DAWG ≈ 1 LIBRE of backing.

In practice this means that DAWG is sold only for LIBRE on the Stellar DEX (SDEX), and the LIBRE collected flows into a Treasury that can be used for:

  • building & supporting DAWG/LIBRE and DAWG/XLM liquidity,
  • strengthening the backing per DAWG over time,
  • and potentially burning LIBRE or DAWG as part of long-term policy.

Important: DAWG is a work-in-progress experimental design. The exact tokenomics and long-term reserve rules are still being refined. There is no guarantee of redemption, profit, or fixed price – only a transparent attempt to build a meme-denominated reserve-style asset rooted in LIBRE and the Stellar ecosystem.

Current State & Distribution Principles

For now, new DAWG enters the market only through limit sell offers against LIBRE on the SDEX. There is no public sale in another currency and no presale priced in fiat or stablecoins.

As part of the early design, the goal is:

  • to keep DAWG’s max supply capped at 5,000,000 DAWG,
  • to release supply gradually in small tranches,
  • and to only expand circulating supply when the Treasury backing is considered sufficient.

Everything here is subject to change based on actual on-chain behavior, community feedback, and future smart-contract based implementations on Soroban.
