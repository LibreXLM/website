Over the last few years I have created a growing set of tokens that together form the LibreTradingEcosystem. Many of these assets trade permanently against LIBRE, against other tokens on the Stellar network, and in some cases even against other personal trading tokens inside this ecosystem.

The names of these tokens can be read in different ways: sometimes they are completely random or playful, sometimes they are a subtle hint towards the underlying liquidity composition, the idea behind the token, or the way it is meant to move in relation to other assets. A weird name does not necessarily mean ‚Äújust a meme‚Äù ‚Äì it might be wired into a specific liquidity setup.

All of these tokens are experimental and risky. Their performance and value depend on:

  • the underlying liquidity pool composition (which assets, and in what proportions),
  • accumulated and future trading fees inside AMM pools,
  • and any external demand or interest from other users.

Nothing here is financial advice. The LibreTradingEcosystem is an open-ended experiment in on-chain motion, programmable value, and self-built micro economies on Stellar. If you interact with these tokens, you do so at your own risk.
